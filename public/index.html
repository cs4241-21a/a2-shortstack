How to Play

Enter a name and Elemental Strength for a new fighter in The Pit. Slide the bar to choose how much Power (extra damage) and Agility (chance of dodging hits) they will have, then click the button.

Then, your fighter will be randomly matched against an opponent in the roster to the right. They will try to hit each other 10 times (factoring in the variables you set, plus derived ones like Weakness) - the battle ends if one combatant's health runs out or the 10 turns pass. The new fighter is then added to the roster, and the other combatant's survival/defeat data is updated.

  • -1 damage if attacker's Strength = defender's Strength
  • +1 damage if attacker's Strength = defender's Weakness
  • Attacker's Power is added to the base damage of 1
  • Chance of defender dodging an attack = 10% * Agility
