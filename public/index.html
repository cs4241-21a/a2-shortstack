WELCOM TO TETRIS!

Enter your name and press "Let's Game" to start the game.
After your game is over the application would update the scoreboard.
The game controls are as follows:

  • S - move Tetris piece down faster
  • A - move Tetris piece left
  • D - move Tetris piece right
  • Q - rotate Tetris piece counter-clockwise
  • E - rotate Tetris piece clockwise
