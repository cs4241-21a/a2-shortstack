Note

To add a new entry fill in the fields, and click update.
To modify an entry select the row on the table, update the fields, and click update.
To delete an entry click delete on the desired row.
Click the table once to focus on the table, then click the desired row for modifying/deleting.
Entries must have unique names. Adding/modifying an entry with the same name as an existing one will replace the previous entry.
Gift selected with no birthday will default to the Jan. 1st of the coming year.
